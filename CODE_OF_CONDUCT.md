# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and seek clarity.
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not people.
- Help create an inclusive community for contributors of all backgrounds.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks, insults, or abusive language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional open-source project.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for interpreting and enforcing this policy. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

To report behavior that violates this policy, open a private security advisory at:

https://github.com/qonhq/qon-js/security/advisories/new

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.
